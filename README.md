🐞 Supportive Insect

A tiny companion that lives in your browser and checks in on you every day.

一只住在浏览器里的小瓢虫，每天陪你说说话。

→ [Try it live](https://enso-12.github.io/supportive-insect/)

What is this?

A mood tracking web app disguised as a cute ladybug. 

Tap the bug, it talks to you. 

Record your mood, write a few words about your day, and look back at your mood calendar over time.

No account needed. 

No data uploaded. 

Everything stays on your device.

这是一个伪装成可爱瓢虫的心情记录工具。

点一下小虫子，它会跟你说话。

记录每天的心情，写几句话，回头看看自己这个月过得怎么样。

不需要注册，不上传数据，所有内容保存在你的设备上。

Features

🐞 Tap the bug for encouragement

😊 Daily mood tracking (happy / okay / tired / sad / upset)

📝 Short journal entries

📅 Mood calendar — see your month at a glance

🌏 Trilingual — 中文 / 日本語 / English

🌙 Dreamy night sky UI

📱 Works on phone — add to home screen for app-like experience


Tech：
Pure HTML + CSS + JS. 
No framework, 
no backend, 
no dependencies. 
Data stored in localStorage.
Privacy：
Zero tracking. 
Zero analytics. 
Zero data collection. 
Your mood is yours.
